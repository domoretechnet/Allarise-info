# Wake up smarter.

Tired of hitting snooze? Allarise makes sleeping in a choice, not an accident — and brings your whole smart home along for the ride.

Solve a math problem or shake your phone to dismiss — no more dismissing your alarm while you sleep. Deep Home Assistant integration means your lights, routines, and automations can respond to every alarm event on your phone. One fair price — no subscription required.

---

## Features

Everything your alarm should be.

Built for people who are serious about waking up — and smart home enthusiasts who want their alarm integrated with everything else.

### Alarm Engine
Alarms that mean business. Allarise's alarm engine plays at the volume you set, independent of your ringer and mute switch — so a low ringer volume won't ruin your morning. A system-level backup is also registered automatically as a failsafe if the app is ever closed, though it uses iOS ringer volume.

### Home Assistant Integration
Connect via MQTT to your Home Assistant instance. Trigger automations when your alarm fires, snoozes, or is dismissed — and send commands back.

### Sleep Sounds
Fall asleep to rain, ocean waves, white noise, and more. Lock screen controls, sleep timers, and plays on any speaker.

### Wake-Up Missions
Dismiss your alarm by solving a math problem, shaking your phone, or triggering a Home Assistant action — no more oversleeping.

### Wallpaper Packs
Choose from curated wallpaper packs that transform your home and alarm screens. Each pack includes light and dark variants designed to look great out of the box.

### Morning Weather
See today's weather overview on your last alarm screen — get an at-a-glance forecast the moment you wake up, powered by Apple WeatherKit.

### Privacy First
No analytics, no ads, no account required. All data stays on your device. MQTT credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain.

The only external communication is with Apple's native services (WeatherKit, the App Store) and — if you configure it — your own self-hosted Home Assistant MQTT broker. Read the full privacy policy.
