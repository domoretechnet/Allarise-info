App Features
⏰

Alarm Engine Pro

Alarms that mean business. Allarise plays at the volume you set, independent of your ringer and mute switch.

Free includes 2 alarms. Pro unlocks unlimited.

🏠

Home Assistant Integration

Connect via MQTT to your Home Assistant instance. Trigger automations when your alarm fires, snoozed, or dismissed. Two-way commands let Home Assistant talk back.

🌙

Sleep Sounds Pro

Fall asleep to rain, ocean waves, white noise, and more. Lock screen controls, sleep timers, and controllable remotely from Home Assistant.

🧩

Wake-Up Missions

Dismiss your alarm by solving a math problem, shaking your phone, or triggering a Home Assistant action. Built for people who actually need to wake up.

🎨

Wallpaper Packs Pro

Curated wallpaper packs that transform your home and alarm screens. Each pack includes light and dark variants designed to look great out of the box.

🌤

Morning Weather

See today's forecast the moment you wake up, powered by Apple WeatherKit. Weather can be enabled on up to two alarms.

🔒

Privacy First

No analytics, no ads, no account required. All data stays on your device. MQTT credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain.

📊

Sensors

Alarm state, name, fire time, snooze count, app version, sleep sound volume, and more.

🔘

Buttons

Dismiss, snooze, skip, unskip, and kill snoozed — available on the dashboard device and each per-alarm device.

🔁

Switches

Master arm toggle, per-alarm enable/disable, alert vibrate, and alert media loop.

🔊

Media Player

Play audio or TTS directly to the phone via play_media and media_announce.

🚨

Notify

Send a full-screen alert to the phone from any HA automation using the notify service.

⏰

Per-Alarm Devices

Each alarm gets its own HA device with full control — time, days, sound, snooze, and more.

🌙

Sleep Sounds

Start, stop, pause, or change white noise and ambient sounds on the phone remotely from any automation.

🔐

Arm Widget

A switch entity that syncs with the app's arm toggle — use it to tell Home Assistant when your home security is armed.

⚡

Commands

Fire named commands from the app's alarm screen to trigger HA automations — dismiss, snooze, swipe actions, and custom widget buttons.

No data collected. Allarise is built local-first. Your alarms and settings never leave your device. The app only communicates with Apple's native services such as WeatherKit and the App Store, and with your own self-hosted Home Assistant MQTT broker if you enable integration.
