Wake up smarter.

Your alarms shouldn't just wake you up — they should wake up your whole house.

Allarise syncs with Home Assistant so your lights, routines, and automations can respond when your alarm goes off. Wake up to a house that's already moving, and stay accountable with built-in missions like math and shake.

With full two-way MQTT, your alarms trigger automations, and your automations control your alarms. If you run Home Assistant, Allarise is the alarm your setup has been missing.

Alarm Engine Pro

Alarms that mean business. Allarise plays at the volume you set, independent of your ringer and mute switch.

Home Assistant Integration

Full two-way MQTT integration with Home Assistant. Your alarms publish state as sensors, buttons trigger automations, and HA can send commands right back — create alarms, control sleep sounds, arm zones, and much more.
🌙

Sleep Sounds

Fall asleep to rain, ocean waves, white noise, and more. Lock screen controls, sleep timers, and controllable remotely from Home Assistant.

🧩

Wake-Up Missions

Dismiss your alarm by solving a math problem, shaking your phone, or triggering a Home Assistant action. Built for people who actually need to wake up.

🎨

Wallpaper Packs Pro

Curated wallpaper packs that transform your home and alarm screens. Each pack includes light and dark variants designed to look great out of the box.

🌤

Morning Weather

See today's forecast the moment you wake up, powered by Apple WeatherKit. Weather can be enabled on up to two alarms.

🔒

Privacy First

No analytics, no ads, no account required. All data stays on your device. MQTT credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain.
