Features

Everything your alarm should be.

Built from the ground up for people who are serious about waking up. Perfect for smart home users who want their alarm connected to everything.

⏰

Alarm Engine Pro*

Alarms that mean business. Allarise plays at the volume you set, independent of your ringer and mute switch.

🏠

Home Assistant Integration

Connect via MQTT to your Home Assistant instance. Trigger automations when your alarm fires, snoozed, or dismissed. Two-way commands let Home Assistant talk back. This level of alarm integration is virtually unheard of on iOS.

🌙

Sleep Sounds Pro

Fall asleep to rain, ocean waves, white noise, and more. Lock screen controls, sleep timers, and can be controlled remotely from Home Assistant.

🧩

Wake-Up Missions

Dismiss your alarm by solving a math problem, shaking your phone, or triggering a Home Assistant action. Built for people who actually need to wake up.

🎨

Wallpaper Packs Pro

Choose from curated wallpaper packs that transform your home and alarm screens. Each pack includes light and dark variants designed to look great out of the box.

🌤

Morning Weather

See today's weather on your final alarm screen for an at-a-glance forecast the moment you wake up, powered by Apple WeatherKit. Weather can be enabled on up to two alarms.

🔒

Privacy First

No analytics, no ads, no account required. All data stays on your device. MQTT credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain.

* Free includes 2 alarms. Pro unlocks unlimited.
